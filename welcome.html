Welcome aboard

Here's how it works.

Two minutes now saves you twenty later. Everything in Notiq flows through one simple loop — bring material in, turn it into study tools, then master it.

1

Add material — or just pick a topic

Press + Add material or learn a topic on your dashboard. Drop in a PDF, paste your notes, add a YouTube lecture link — or simply type anything you want to learn (or browse by subject) and Notiq writes the study material for you and names it automatically.

2

Make a folder

In the Folders row on your dashboard, hit New folder, name it after your class (like "AP Biology"), and pick a color. Folders keep everything you save organized.

3

Generate your study tools

From the dashboard, pick Flashcards, Quiz, Test, Study guide, or Notes. Select your resources, hit generate, and — this part matters — press Save to folder on anything you want to keep. Nothing saves automatically.

4

Master it, don't just read it

Once a deck is saved, the power tools open up: Smart review brings cards back right before you'd forget them, Match, Blitz and Sprint turn studying into games, and Teach it back grades your own explanations. The Focus timer keeps you honest — leave the tab and it fails you.

5

Crunch time? There's a plan for that

Study guide asks when your exam is and plans every day until then. Cram mode squeezes everything onto one high-yield page when time is short. And any test, deck or notes can be printed or read aloud with Listen.

6

Make it yours

Click your avatar at the bottom of the sidebar to pick from 32 color palettes — everything recolors, even the home page. Study any day to grow your streak, and use the chat bubble icon in the sidebar to send feedback whenever something bugs you.
