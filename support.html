Before you dive in

A quick, honest note.

Notiq AI has no funding. No company behind it, no investors, no ads. It's built by a student, and right now every single flashcard, quiz, and study guide you generate costs real money that comes straight out of one student's pocket.

And it's not just the AI. The API keys that power every generation, the database that keeps your decks and folders safe, the file storage, the hosting — all of it bills us every single month, whether anyone donates or not. Keeping a site like this alive costs real money before a single new feature ever gets built. That's why we need your help right now.

That's also why the AI we can afford today isn't the biggest or fastest one out there. Most of the time you won't notice. But sometimes a generation will be slow, or fail, or the site will tell you too many people are on at once. That's not carelessness — that's the honest ceiling of what we can pay for today. This is a beta — we're handing it to people to try out, rough edges and all, because we'd rather help students now than wait for perfect.

If something breaks, tell us.

We're a tiny operation, which means we can't see every problem — but we read every single piece of feedback. If a generation fails, if something looks wrong, if anything at all gives you trouble, please hit the chat bubble icon in the sidebar and tell us. It takes thirty seconds, and it genuinely shapes what gets fixed first. You reporting a bug tonight is the reason it's gone tomorrow.

Students helping students.

Here's the whole idea behind this site: good study tools shouldn't depend on how much money you have. Some of the best tools out there lock everything useful behind a subscription that a lot of students simply can't pay. We think that's backwards. The student who can't afford the subscription is usually the one who needs the help most.

So Notiq's Free plan stays free — real studying, every day, no card needed. What pays the bills is Pro: $4.99 a month for everything unlimited — generations, resources, folders, saves, all of it. 100% of that money goes straight back into the site: a stronger AI model, more generations for everyone, and new features. Not a cent goes anywhere else. Students helping students — that's the entire business plan. (Prefer a one-time tip? Donations are always welcome.)

How you can help today.

Use the site hard. Break it, and tell us how you broke it through the feedback bubble. Tell a friend who has a test coming up. Be a little patient when it's busy. And if Notiq has ever saved you a late night, going Pro for $4.99/mo — or even a one-time donation — keeps the lights on for the next student.

Our mission

Right now Notiq is a beta we're giving away so students can try it. But here's the guarantee: the moment funding comes in, every cent goes into making this website perfect — a stronger AI, faster generations, no limits, no downtime. The best free study tool on the internet, built by students, for students. That's the whole mission.
