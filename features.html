Features

Everything Notiq AI does.

One place where your notes, slides and lectures become a full study system. Here's every feature, in detail — so you know exactly what you're getting before you make an account. All of it is free to start.

Bring in your material
R

Resources

Your material lives in one place. Upload PDFs, text files or Markdown, or just paste text straight in — lecture notes, a chapter, anything. Notiq reads the text out of your files automatically.

Everything you generate — flashcards, quizzes, guides, notes — is built from the resources you pick, so it's always about your class, not generic content.

Y

YouTube import

Paste a link to any YouTube video that has captions and Notiq pulls the full transcript in as a resource. A 40-minute lecture becomes something you can turn into flashcards, a quiz or clean notes in seconds.

Generate your study tools
F

Flashcards

Pick your resources and how many cards you want. Decks ramp from easy to hard — definitions first, tricky applications last — with a difficulty badge on every card.

Flip cards to study, edit any card you want to reword, and save the deck into a folder.

Q

Quizzes

Multiple-choice questions written from your material, each with four options and an explanation for the answer. Take the quiz right on the page and get your score instantly.

After checking answers, Notiq lists your weak spots — every question you missed — and can generate a fresh practice quiz attacking exactly those concepts.

S

Study guide

Pick your exam day on a calendar and the resources you need to know. Notiq plans every day between now and then: what to learn, what to review from earlier days, and quick self-checks — with a light review day right before the exam.

N

Notes

Turn any resource into clean, detailed, beautifully structured notes — headings, bolded key terms, examples and comparison tables, ending with a quick summary of the most important takeaways. Usually better than what you started with.

C

Cram mode

Short on time? Cram mode condenses your material into one dense page: the 10 must-know ideas, key terms, commonly-confused pairs, and the 5 most likely exam questions with model answers. A lot of information into your head, fast.

Actually master it
T

Teach it back

The reverse of a quiz, based on the Feynman technique. Notiq picks a concept from your material and asks you to explain it in your own words. Then it grades you out of 10 — what you nailed, what you got wrong or skipped, and one concrete tip.

Struggling to explain something is the fastest way to find out you don't really know it yet.

SR

Smart review

Real spaced repetition. Saved decks come back to you on a schedule: answer "Got it" and a card returns in 2, 4, 8, 16 days; answer "Again" and it's back tomorrow. The dashboard tells you how many cards are due each day.

%

Mastery bars

Every folder shows a mastery percentage that grows as your review intervals stretch out. Watch "AP Bio — 40% mastered" climb toward 100 as exam day approaches.

Stay on track
FT

Focus timer

Pick 15, 25 or 45 minutes and lock in. Leave the tab for more than 30 seconds and the session fails — the timer keeps you honest. Finished sessions count toward your streak.

D

Daily streak

Study any day — open the app, generate something, review cards — and your streak grows. Miss a day and it resets. Simple, and weirdly motivating.

TP

Tip of the day

A rotating test-taking tip on your dashboard every day — recall practice, spacing, test-day tactics. Small habits that add up to real marks.

Your library
FL

Folders

Organize saved flashcards, quizzes, study guides, notes and cram sheets into folders — by class, by unit, however you like. Nothing saves unless you hit Save to folder, so your library stays intentional.

SH

Share a folder

One click gives you a link anyone can open — no account needed — to view a folder's decks, quizzes and notes read-only. Perfect for study groups. Turn sharing off anytime and the link dies instantly.

Take it anywhere
L

Read it to me

A Listen button on flashcards, notes, study guides and cram sheets reads them aloud with your browser's voice. Study on a walk, on the bus, or with your eyes closed.

P

Print & export

Everything can leave the app. Print any deck, quiz, guide or notes as a clean PDF — quizzes print with a separate answer key so you can test on paper. Flashcard decks also export as CSV, ready to import into Quizlet or Anki.
